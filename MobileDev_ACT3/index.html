Device Catalog Instructions

Manage Your Devices and Favorites

  • Enter a device name in the input field under "Add Device".
  • Click the "Add Device" button to add the device to the catalog.
  • To add a device to your favorites, click the "Favorite" button next to the device in the catalog.
  • To remove a device from your favorites, click the "Delete" button next to the device in the favorites list.
  • To clear all favorites, click the "Clear All Favorites" button.
